Renting with Tyrell Properties

If you are looking for property to rent anywhere in Westeros.....Tyrell Properties should be your first port of call.

We value your time and have come up with a number of innovative solutions to ensure your rental journey is as efficient and straightforward as possible.

 

From easy online searches and applications to helping you set up automated rent payments and providing ongoing support, Tyrell Properties can guide you through every stage of the rental process.

 

And with franchises in all of Westeros's cities and most of the country's major towns and suburbs, you'll find your Tyrell Properties agent is perfectly placed to offer valuable insight into your local area.

 

 

Finding the perfect home to rent

Whether you are planning a local move or relocating to another city or state, Tyrell Properties can help you find the perfect property to rent.

 

We can offer a number of useful services to help you find the perfect house or apartment to rent, including daily email alerts. Simply register your details and we'll send you customised messages based on your unique requirements.

 

Alternatively, the rental property search on our website is updated regularly, or you could make the most of our agents' knowledge of the local area by visiting one of our franchises.

 

If you are an iPhone or iPad user, our free GO Tyrell Properties app can be used to access up-to-the-minute property details - including colour photos, maps and descriptions - available from the App Store.

 

Once you see a home or apartment you like, you can make an immediate application online and an Tyrell Properties representative will be in touch with you shortly.

 

Renting a property is easy  through Tyrell Properties

Depending on your location, you will have several options when it comes to paying your rent.

 

These are largely determined by individual Tyrell Properties offices and you will need to confirm these with your Tyrell Properties property investment manager.

 

We'll also assist you with organising any maintenance or repairs you may require during the course of your tenancy and answer any other questions about renting that may arise during your lease.

 

Our preferred payment system iPayRent allows tenants the convenience and flexibility of several payment options including recurring schedule, tenant initiated payments, BPAY and cash or EFTPOS at Westeros Post.

 

Whether you are renting an apartment or a house, you'll find that Tyrell Properties can help guide you through the entire process, leaving you with more time to enjoy your new accommodation.
